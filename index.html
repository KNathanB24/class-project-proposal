Class Project Proposal

  1. Product Name: Projectile Motion Interactive Physics Simulator
  2. Team Members: Kyle Bledsoe
  3. Application Overview: A web based physics simulator that will allow students to practice understanding the concept of projectile motion. Students will be able to adjust physical parameters like gravity, height, angles, mass, and much more, and will be able to see a physical representation of the affects these variables have on a projectile motion system.
